How to Play Word Frames

Welcome to Word Frames, a challenging word puzzle game!

Objective: Create words using the given letters in a 5x5 grid.

Gameplay:

  • Click on empty grid spaces to place the current letter.
  • Form words of 2-5 letters horizontally (left to right) or vertically (top to bottom).
  • Words can overlap and share letters.
  • Each letter can only be used once.

Scoring:

  • 2-letter words: 2 points
  • 3-letter words: 3 points
  • 4-letter words: 4 points
  • 5-letter words: 5 points

Tips:

  • Plan ahead! Look for potential word combinations before placing letters.
  • Try to create multiple words with each placement.
  • Keep an eye on your high score and try to beat it!

Good luck and have fun expanding your vocabulary!
